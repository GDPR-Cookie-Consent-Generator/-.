How to Use the GDPR Cookie Consent Generator

Creating and implementing a GDPR-compliant cookie consent banner for your website is simple with our generator. Follow these easy steps:

1

Customize Your Banner

Use the customization options on our homepage to design your cookie consent banner:

  • Banner Position: Choose whether your banner appears at the top or bottom of your website.
  • Background Color: Select a background color that matches your website's design.
  • Text Color: Choose a text color that provides good contrast with your background.
  • Button Text: Customize the text for your Accept and Reject buttons.
  • Privacy Policy URL: Add the link to your privacy policy page.
  • Banner Text: Modify the text that explains your cookie usage to visitors.

As you make changes, the live preview will update to show you exactly how your banner will look.

2

Generate Your Code

Once you're happy with your design:

  1. Click the "Generate Code" button.
  2. Review the generated HTML and JavaScript code.

3

Implement on Your Website

To add the cookie consent banner to your website:

  1. Access your website's HTML files (through your CMS, hosting provider, or FTP).
  2. Paste the code before the closing </body> tag on every page where you want the banner to appear.
  3. Save your changes and publish or upload the updated files.

Your HTML structure should look something like this:

<html>
  <head>
    <!-- Your existing head content -->
  </head>
  <body>
    <!-- Your website content -->
    
    <!-- Paste the generated code here -->
    <div id="gdpr-cookie-consent">...</div>
    <script>...</script>
  </body>
</html>
4

Test Your Banner

After implementation:

  1. Visit your website in different browsers and devices to ensure the banner displays correctly.
  2. Test the Accept and Reject buttons to verify they work as expected.
  3. Check that the "Learn more" link directs users to your privacy policy page.

The cookie consent banner should appear when a new visitor accesses your site and disappear once they accept or reject cookies.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does this make my website fully GDPR compliant?

The cookie consent banner is just one aspect of GDPR compliance. Full compliance involves several other measures, including having a comprehensive privacy policy, proper data processing agreements, and appropriate data handling practices. We recommend consulting with a legal professional for complete GDPR compliance advice.

Will this work with any website?

Yes, our generated code is designed to work with any HTML website. It uses vanilla JavaScript and should be compatible with most modern websites, including those built with WordPress, Shopify, Wix, and other platforms.

How does the cookie consent actually work?

When a user clicks "Accept," the script sets a cookie in their browser recording their consent. When they click "Reject," it sets a different cookie recording their rejection. These cookies are then used to determine whether to load optional cookies/trackers in the future. The banner will not show again to users who have already made a choice.

Can I modify the code after generating it?

Absolutely! The generated code is provided as a starting point. You're free to modify it to better suit your needs or to add additional functionality. Just make sure any changes maintain GDPR compliance.

I need more help. What should I do?

If you need assistance implementing the code or have other questions, feel free to contact us at support@gdprcookieconsentgenerator.com. For legal advice regarding GDPR compliance, please consult with a qualified legal professional.
