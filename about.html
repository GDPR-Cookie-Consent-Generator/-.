About Me

Web Developer & GDPR Compliance Specialist

Hi there! I'm John, a 26-year-old web developer passionate about helping website owners comply with GDPR regulations quickly and efficiently.

I created this GDPR Cookie Consent Generator tool after realizing how many small business owners and independent developers struggle with implementing proper GDPR compliance. Many existing solutions are either expensive, require signups, or are too complex for the average user.

My mission is to make the web a more privacy-respecting place by providing free, accessible tools that enable website owners to comply with regulations without the hassle or expense. I believe that privacy protection should be available to everyone, not just those who can afford premium services.

My Background

With over 5 years of experience in web development and a specialization in privacy compliance, I've helped numerous businesses implement proper data protection measures. I hold certifications in GDPR compliance and regularly keep up with the latest developments in privacy regulations.
